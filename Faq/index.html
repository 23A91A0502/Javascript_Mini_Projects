Frequently Asked Questions

What is the return policy?
We offer a 30-day refund policy for all unused and unopened products. Customers may return items for a full refund within 30 days of purchase, provided they are in their original condition and packaging.
What will I receive my order?
You can expect to receive your order based on the shipping and delivery timeframe provided in your order confirmation email, which often includes an estimated delivery date or a range.

The location they are asking about is the store's delivery address, or the specific store branch from which the product will be shipped.
